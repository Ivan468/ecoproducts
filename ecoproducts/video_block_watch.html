Not only do we get our products lab tested for compostability, we also test them out in the real world. We worked together with A1 Organics and the Compost Manufacturing Alliance (CMA) to confirm that our products break down as we intend into usable compost.

This type of collaboration between the composting industry and the compostable products industry on real-world field testing is critical to ensure that the products brought to our compost partners will actually break down at their sites.
